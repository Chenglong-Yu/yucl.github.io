    Generalizable Detection of AI-Generated Videos

    Investigated video-forensics methods for unseen generators and low-data settings, with an emphasis on cross-generator generalization, robustness to real-world video degradation, and lightweight deployment.

    Video Forensics Zero/Few-Shot Learning Diffusion Models Meta-Learning Knowledge Distillation
